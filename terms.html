When you book, you’re reserving a time slot. If you need to reschedule, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.

Cancellations and refunds

Because this is a small service, missed sessions can be difficult to fill. I aim to be fair about it, while protecting the schedule.

  • No-shows: The session is forfeited. No refund or reschedule.
